About Afghani Almonds

Afghani almonds are harvested from the mountainous and dry regions of Afghanistan. The climate there is naturally cold, sunlight is strong, and humidity is low — is perfect environment mein almonds dheere dheere grow hote hain, jisse unka flavour strong, crunch natural aur aroma deep ho jaata hai.

Where They Come From

Hamare Afghani almonds direct trusted Afghan farms se source kiye jaate hain. Har batch ko size, colour, crunch aur moisture ke hisaab se check kiya jata hai. Low-grade ya broken almonds ko reject kiya jata hai — sirf premium grade almonds A1 quality ke liye select hote hain.

How We Handle & Deliver

India aane ke baad almonds ko dobara clean aur sort kiya jata hai. Hum airtight, food-grade packing use karte hain taaki almonds moisture, heat aur dust se safe rahein. No polishing, no artificial colour — 100% natural Afghani almonds.
